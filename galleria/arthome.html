My name is Andi.
I'm a 25 year old indie artist based in Los Angeles.

I like to take on different forms of mediums like pixel art, comics and animation. I try to experiment often so you may notice nothing is consistent. As I continue to grow and improve, my styles get more solid and accurate to my self expression.
I feel that a lot of my old work isn't reflective of my work now but I think it should still be viewable.

Thank you so much for viewing my work!
